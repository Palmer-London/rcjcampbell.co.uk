Welcome

You are by now aware of the sad news of the death of Robert Campbell. We are launching this website both to deliver information and to invite you to share your thoughts with us. At this stage the site will provide information for the arrangements but over time we will share memories, celebrate Robert's life and we invite you to visit frequently, contribute and help us create a lasting tribute to a singular individual.

You are invited to a short service at Falkirk Crematorium. Family flowers only please. If you wish a donation can be sent to the Dogs Trust, Robert's favourite charity. Please register if you are intending to join as this will be helpful with regards to numbers both at the service and/or afterwards at Stirling Management Centre.

We will all be meeting later at Stirling Management Centre in the grounds of Stirling University. You are invited to join us for a less formal gathering and refreshment. Please let us know by registering if you are able to join us.
